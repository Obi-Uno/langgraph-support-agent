# Shipping Policy

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the country. Expedited shipping
(2-day) is available at checkout for an additional $15.

Once an order status is "shipped", it cannot be cancelled, only returned after
delivery following the standard return policy.

If a package is marked delivered but the customer says they never received it,
open a support ticket — do not promise a reshipment or refund without human review.
